Type Of Advance Zirconia

Monolithic Zirconia Crown

Monolithic zirconia is a single-piece, durable dental material known for its strength, aesthetics, and longevity. It's often used for crowns and bridges, offering a smooth, translucent, and natural-looking finish that mimics real teeth.

Multi-Layered Zirconia

Multilayered zirconia is a dental material with varying translucency, designed to mimic natural tooth structure. It combines strength, durability, and excellent aesthetics, making it the preferred choice for advanced zirconia implants due to its lifelike appearance.

Zirconia Implant

Zirconia implants are advanced ceramic dental implants made from zirconium dioxide,45 offering exceptional strength and a natural, tooth-like appearance. They blend seamlessly with the surrounding teeth and can last 20-25 years with proper care.

E Max Layered Zirconia

EMax layered zirconia is an advanced dental material that combines the strength of zirconia with the natural beauty of porcelain. It offers outstanding durability, lifelike aesthetics, and excellent wear resistance. Choose eMax layered zirconia for visible teeth or high-stress areas where both beauty and strength are essential.
