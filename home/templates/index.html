Our Core Dental Services for Crowns, Bridges, and Implants

Experience an Unmatched Spectrum of Services: Catering to Every Dental Professional's Needs Today!

CAD/CAM Technology

We support 3M True Definition, Cadent iTero, and other open systems to deliver accurate, digitally designed restorations. Our team is here to assist with seamless digital case submissions.

Shade Analysis

We offer precise shade matching through digital photos or in-lab consultations. For best results, send images to: advancedentalexport@gmail.com

Case Planning Assistance

We offer one-on-one consultations to help plan cases with confidence, providing technical insights and treatment recommendations tailored to your clinical goals.

Diagnostic Wax-Ups

We offer detailed diagnostic wax-ups to support effective treatment planning and patient communication.

Cosmetic Dentistry

We create aesthetic diagnostic wax-ups and silicone matrices for mock-ups and temporaries. Using advanced materials and techniques, we deliver consistent, natural-looking results every time.

Implant Specialists

We support implant cases with diagnostic wax-ups, stent fabrication, and implant components. Custom abutments and denture bars are designed in-house for precision and reliability.

Custom Milling

We provide precision milling for zirconia, implant abutments, overdenture bars, temporaries, and ceramics, crafted in-house for quality and accuracy.

Laser Welding

Instead of replacing full prosthetics, we support modern restorative dentistry with quick, accurate laser welding of metal frameworks and components.
