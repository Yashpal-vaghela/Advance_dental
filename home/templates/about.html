Welcome to Advance Dental Export

Best Dental Lab Services & Leader in Dental Implant Prosthesis, Cosmetic Dentistry and Digital Dentistry

At Advance Dental Export (ADE), we don’t just craft dental prosthetics—we redefine smiles with precision, aesthetics, and longevity.

Founded in 2009 by our visionary CEO, Haresh Savani, ADE has become a trusted leader in dental implant prosthesis, cosmetic dentistry, and digital dentistry. Every restoration we create is meticulously crafted in-house, ensuring faster turnaround times, minimal adjustments, and long-lasting results.

Embracing the Digital Revolution in Dentistry

Digital Dentistry is no longer the future—it’s the present. As pioneers in CAD/CAM digital solutions, we deliver unparalleled accuracy, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness.

🔹 Instant 3D Scanning & Digital Workflow – Say goodbye to traditional impressions!

🔹 Faster Turnaround, Superior Precision – Your patients deserve the best, without a wait

🔹 Seamless Integration with Leading CAD/CAM Scanners – We support Cerec Connect, 3Shape, iTero, 3M ESPE, Carestream, and more.

🔹 State-of-the-Art Milling & Design – Customized prosthetics with impeccable esthetics

As one of the only laboratories in the industry to guarantee restorations using authentic manufacturer’s hardware and custom CAD/CAM abutments, we ensure your implants retain their full warranty.

Quality Dental Services by ADE

CAD/CAM Technology

Experience cutting-edge digital precision with Advance Dental Export. We support 3M True Definition, Cadent iTero, and all major open systems.

Cosmetic Restoration

Achieve flawless, natural-looking results with esthetic wax-ups, silicone matrices, and premium restorative materials for predictable success.

Implant Specialists

From diagnostic wax-ups to custom abutments, we provide expert implant case planning, stent fabrication, and precision denture bars.

Custom Milling

We craft zirconia, implant abutments, overdenture bars, temporaries, and ceramics with state-of-the-art digital milling technology.

Laser Welding

Need quick, precise repairs? Our advanced laser welding technology ensures seamless fixes for partial dentures and restorations.

Diagnostic Wax-Ups

Plan with confidence using our expertly crafted diagnostic wax-ups, providing precise treatment insights for superior patient outcomes.

Case Planning Assistance

Receive personalized case consultations with expert technical recommendations to enhance treatment efficiency and precision.

Shade Analysis

Perfect your shade match with digital photos or in-lab consultations email us at advancedentalexport@gmail.com for expert assistance.
